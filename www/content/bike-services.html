Fitting
The proper fit of a bicycle is more than just being able to reach the pedals and the handlebar. Achieving a comfortable riding position will alleviate fatigue and discomfort during long rides and give the rider better control over the handling of the bicycle. The fit area allows you to have the bicycle adjusted in a dynamic environment and is an essential step in selecting a new bicycle.
